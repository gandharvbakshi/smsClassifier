Delete SMS Classifier data

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This page explains how users of the SMS Classifier Android app can request deletion of app data.

Delete data inside the app

  1. Open SMS Classifier.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Privacy & Data.
  4. Tap Delete my data.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

This deletes SMS Classifier's local message database and sends a deletion request to our backend for uploaded feedback and account-recovery identifiers tied to your install.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email gandharv@musicaigeneration.com with the subject line Delete my SMS Classifier data.

If possible, include your SMS Classifier install id from Settings > About. This helps us find uploaded feedback or Pro account-recovery records tied to your install.

Data deleted

  • Local SMS Classifier message database and classification labels on your device.
  • Uploaded misclassification feedback tied to your install id.
  • Firebase Authentication UID and phone-number account-recovery link, if you used optional Pro recovery.
  • Backend entitlement records tied to your install id or Firebase UID, except purchase records that must be kept for billing, tax, fraud-prevention, or legal reasons.

Data kept

  • Google Play subscription and purchase records required to process billing, cancellation, restore, tax, fraud-prevention, and legal obligations.
  • Already aggregated analytics that cannot reasonably identify you.
  • Already-trained model outputs. Deleting a report prevents future direct access to that report, but does not automatically retrain older models.

We action email deletion requests within 14 days. Uninstalling the app removes local app data from your device, but it does not remove data previously uploaded to our backend. Use the in-app deletion flow or email us if you need server-side deletion too.

For full privacy details, read the SMS Classifier Privacy Policy.
