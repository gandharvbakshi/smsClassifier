# Product Context - SMS Classifier

SMS Classifier is an Android SMS inbox and warning helper for everyday SMS users and family buyers who worry about OTP fraud, scam-like texts, and confusing service messages.

## Category

Consumer SMS warning app / safer SMS inbox.

## Target Profiles

- Everyday SMS user: receives OTPs, delivery messages, bank/service texts, and occasional suspicious prize/KYC messages.
- Family buyer/caregiver: may install or recommend the app for parents or elderly relatives who can be pressured by calls or confusing SMS.

## Core Proof Used

- OTP purpose warning: the app can show when an OTP appears related to account change rather than the story someone tells the user.
- Scam SMS warning: the app highlights suspicious prize/KYC texts before the user taps or shares information.

## Claim Boundaries

Use shows, warns, flags, helps, and review language. Do not claim it blocks every scam, guarantees safety, or knows which OTPs are safe.
