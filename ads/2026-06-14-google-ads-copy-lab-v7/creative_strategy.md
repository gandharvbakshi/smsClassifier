# Creative Strategy

This v7 run tests the copy-first architecture after the Opus audit. The selected hooks are not feature labels; they are human concerns resolved by product agency.

## Selected Hooks

1. **An OTP scam can change your account. This SMS app warns you**
   - Why: OTP fraud is the highest-stakes, most differentiated use case. The proof shows the account-change reveal.
   - Story framework: problem-agitate-solve.

2. **Worried your parents might tap a scam SMS? This app warns them**
   - Why: The caregiver angle makes the buyer/user motivation concrete and avoids KYC jargon.
   - Story framework: caregiver problem -> warning -> relief.

## Rejected Patterns

- `OTP fraud? SMS app warns first`: too telegraphic.
- `Prize SMS asks KYC? App warns first`: unclear first-read language for the documented consumer target.
- `App flags KYC risk`: product-internal mechanism copy.
