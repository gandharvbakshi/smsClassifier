# Product Context

**Product:** SMS Classifier

**Audience:** Android users who want important SMS messages, OTPs, and suspicious texts to be easier to review.

**Campaign goal:** Google App Campaign install creatives for mobile-first placements.

**Approved acquisition hooks:** find OTPs faster, review suspicious texts before acting, make the SMS inbox clearer.

**Sensitive constraints:** no real SMS, no real phone numbers, no real OTPs, no live URLs, no absolute safety guarantees, and no implication that cloud review is always on or always off.
