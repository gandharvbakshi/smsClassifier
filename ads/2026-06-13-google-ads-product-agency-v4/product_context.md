# Product Context - SMS Classifier

SMS Classifier is an Android SMS inbox and classification helper. The current creative test focuses on two generic acquisition principles: the ad must show the product creating the advantage, and videos must reveal both stakes and product action within the first 3 seconds.

## Core Product Proof Used

- OTP purpose hints: the app can show that a code appears related to account change/login/delivery/etc. This is framed as a decision aid, not a guarantee.
- Risk review: the app highlights messages that deserve review before tapping or sharing information.

## Claim Boundaries

Use `shows`, `flags`, `highlights`, `helps`, and `decision aid`. Do not claim it blocks fraud, catches every scam, or guarantees safety.
